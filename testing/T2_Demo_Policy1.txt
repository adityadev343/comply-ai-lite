Company Privacy Policy v1.0

1. Data Collection: We collect customer name, email, phone number, and address to process orders.

2. Data Usage: We use customer data to fulfill orders, send promotional emails, and improve our services.

3. Data Sharing: We share customer data with our logistics partners and payment gateways for order fulfillment.

4. Data Security: We store all data on secure servers with password protection. We take reasonable steps to protect data.

5. Data Retention: We keep customer data indefinitely for record-keeping and analytics purposes.

6. Children: We do not have any specific policy for children. All users can use our service.

7. User Rights: If you wish to access, correct, or delete your data, please email us at support@company.com. We will try to respond within 30 days.

8. Grievances: For any complaints, email us at support@company.com.

9. Breach Notification: In case of a data breach, we will investigate and take necessary action.

10. International Transfer: We use cloud services located in the USA and Singapore.
