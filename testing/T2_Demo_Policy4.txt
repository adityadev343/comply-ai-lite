Comprehensive Data Protection Policy

1. Consent: Consent is obtained through an explicit, affirmative action (e.g., separate tick boxes for each purpose), with clear language in English/Hindi. Consent is specific, informed, and unambiguous. Users may withdraw consent at any time with the same ease.

2. Notice: We provide a detailed notice before or at the time of consent, listing data items, purposes, user rights, and complaint mechanism.

3. Data Minimisation: We collect only data necessary for the specified purpose.

4. Data Quality: We ensure the accuracy and completeness of data used for decisions or disclosures.

5. Security Safeguards: We implement encryption, access controls, and annual third-party security audits.

6. Data Breach: In case of breach, we will immediately notify the Board in the prescribed format and each affected Data Principal.

7. Erasure: We erase personal data upon withdrawal of consent or when the purpose is no longer served (defined as no contact for 3 years). We direct our processors to do the same.

8. DPO: Appointed and based in India. Contact: dpo@company.com. DPO reports to the Board of Directors.

9. Grievance Mechanism: Dedicated grievance officer, response within 7 days. Escalation to Board if not resolved.

10. Significant Data Fiduciary: We conduct periodic Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA) and annual audits.

11. Children: Parental consent is obtained through a verifiable method (e.g., video call with parent + government ID). We do not track, monitor behaviour, or target advertising to children.

12. International Transfer: We do not transfer personal data outside India except to countries notified by the Central Government.

13. Consent Manager: We enable users to give/manage/withdraw consent through a Board-registered Consent Manager. We comply with all obligations regarding Consent Manager accountability.

14. User Rights: Users may request summary, correction, completion, updating, or erasure of their data via our portal.

15. (Missing) We do not explicitly inform users about their duties under Section 15.

16. (Missing) We have not implemented a mechanism for nomination (Section 14).
